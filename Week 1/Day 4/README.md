# Digital Footprint Investigation – Loop Health

## Overview

This repository contains a digital footprint investigation of **Loop Health**, an Indian HealthTech company. The project explores the organization's online presence, official website, social media activity, career footprint, metadata, WHOIS information, and overall digital identity using publicly available sources.

## Objective

- Investigate the digital footprint of Loop Health.
- Analyze the organization's online presence across multiple platforms.
- Study website structure, metadata, and domain information.
- Evaluate the company's visibility, branding, and public digital identity.
- Apply ethical Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) techniques.

## Investigation Methodology

The investigation was conducted using publicly available information from reliable online sources, including:

- Official company website
- LinkedIn
- Instagram
- YouTube
- Search engines
- WHOIS lookup tools

The collected information was analyzed to understand the organization's digital footprint, branding, and online visibility.
