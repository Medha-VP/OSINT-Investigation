# **INTERNET ARCHIVES REPORT** 

## **Historical Website Analysis using the Wayback Machine** 

### **1. Introduction** 

The internet is constantly evolving. Websites are redesigned, new services are introduced, logos change, and old information disappears. Internet archives preserve previous versions of websites, allowing us to study how organizations have evolved over time. 

One of the most widely used archive services is the **Wayback Machine** , developed by the **Internet Archive** . It stores snapshots of websites taken over many years, enabling researchers, cybersecurity professionals, journalists, and students to view historical versions of web pages. 

This report compares archived versions of three websites with their current versions to understand changes in branding, content, functionality, and user experience. 

### **2. Objectives** 

- Understand how websites evolve over time. 

- Learn to use the Wayback Machine. 

- Compare archived and current versions of websites. 

- Identify changes in branding, navigation, and services. 

- Understand the importance of internet archives in digital investigations.

### **4. Website Comparison 1 – Loop Health** 

**Website:** <u>https://www.loophealth.com</u> 

**Archive Year Used:** 2021

### **<u>Archived Website (2021)</u>**

### **7. Overall Analysis** 

The archived websites clearly demonstrate how organizations adapt to technological advancements and changing customer expectations. 

Some common improvements observed include: 

- More modern and responsive website designs. 

- Better navigation and user experience. 

- Expansion of products and services. 

- Stronger visual branding. 

- Increased use of animations and interactive elements. 

- Improved accessibility for mobile devices. 

- More personalized content for users. 

These changes show how companies continuously refine their online presence to remain competitive and improve customer engagement. 

### **8. Importance of Internet Archives** 

Internet archives are valuable tools because they: 

- Preserve historical versions of websites. 

- Help investigators verify past online content. 

- Support cybersecurity and OSINT investigations. 

- Assist journalists and researchers in fact-checking. 

- Recover information from deleted or modified websites. 

- Document the evolution of organizations and technologies. 

### **9. Conclusion** 

The Wayback Machine provides valuable insights into the evolution of websites over time. By comparing archived and current versions of Loop Health, Airbnb, and Spotify, it is evident that organizations continuously improve their digital presence through better design, enhanced functionality, and expanded services. 

Historical website analysis is an essential skill in digital footprint investigations because it helps researchers understand how an organization's online identity has changed over time. It also provides evidence that may no longer be available on the live website, making internet archives an important resource for cybersecurity, digital forensics, journalism, and historical research.
