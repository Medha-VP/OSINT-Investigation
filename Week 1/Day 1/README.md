# OSINT Investigation Report – OpenAI

## Overview

This repository contains an Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) investigation of **OpenAI** conducted using only publicly available information. The investigation focuses on gathering and organizing information about the organization's background, leadership, business operations, funding, and mission while following ethical and legal OSINT practices.

## Objective

- Investigate OpenAI using publicly available sources.
- Collect organizational and business intelligence.
- Analyze publicly accessible information without interacting with restricted systems.
- Demonstrate ethical OSINT research methodology.

## Investigation Scope

The investigation examines the following aspects of OpenAI:

- Company background and evolution
- Leadership and management
- Products and services
- Business structure
- Funding history
- Publicly available organizational information

## Methodology

The investigation was conducted using publicly available information from reliable sources, including:

- Official company website
- Public company announcements
- News articles
- Business information platforms
- Public reports

No private information or unauthorized access was used during this investigation.

## Learning Outcomes

Through this investigation, the following OSINT concepts were applied:

- Open-source information gathering
- Organization profiling
- Public record analysis
- Business intelligence collection
- Ethical OSINT practices
- Information verification and documentation

## Disclaimer

This project was created for educational purposes as part of an Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) learning exercise. All information was collected from publicly available sources, and no confidential or unauthorized data was accessed.
