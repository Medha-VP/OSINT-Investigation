# Domains, WHOIS, Registrar & TLD Analysis

## Overview

This repository contains a practical Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) investigation focused on **domain registration and WHOIS analysis**. The project examines five different domains to understand their registration details, registrars, Top-Level Domains (TLDs), privacy protection mechanisms, and publicly available registration information.

## Objective

- Perform WHOIS lookups on multiple domains.
- Identify registrars, creation dates, and expiry dates.
- Compare different Top-Level Domains (TLDs).
- Analyze WHOIS privacy protection.
- Understand the role of domain registration information in OSINT investigations.

## Investigation Methodology

The investigation involved performing WHOIS lookups on selected domains to collect publicly available registration information. The analysis included:

- Domain registrar identification
- Registration and expiry dates
- Name server information
- Privacy protection status
- Publicly exposed WHOIS fields
- Comparison of different TLD types

The collected information was analyzed to understand domain ownership patterns and the role of WHOIS data in OSINT investigations.

## Key Findings

- Commercial organizations commonly use WHOIS privacy protection to hide registrant information.
- Government domains follow different registration policies with limited public information.
- Technical registration details such as registrars and name servers remain publicly accessible.
- Different TLDs have different registration authorities and management policies.
- WHOIS data provides valuable information for domain and infrastructure analysis.
