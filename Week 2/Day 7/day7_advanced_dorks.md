# **<u>Advanced Google Dorking</u>** 

Company: IBM 

Website: https://www.ibm.com 

## **<u>Objective</u>** 

To perform passive OSINT using advanced Google Dorking techniques on IBM's public web presence. The investigation focuses on identifying publicly indexed resources while following ethical OSINT practices. 

## **<u>Advanced Google Dorks Used</u>** 

|**Google Dork**|**Purpose**|
|---|---|
|**site:ibm.com filetype:pdf intitle:"Annual**<br>**Report"**|Find IBM annual reports in PDF format|
|**site:ibm.com filetype:pdf intext:"Artificial**<br>**Intelligence"**|Search for AI-related PDF documents|
|**site:ibm.com intitle:"index of"**|Look for publicly indexed directories|
|**site:ibm.com inurl:login**|Identify publicly accessible login pages|
|**cache:ibm.com**|View Google's cached version of the homepage|
|**related:ibm.com**|Discover websites similar to IBM|
|**site:ibm.com filetype:ppt OR filetype:pptx**|Find public PowerPoint presentations|
|**site:ibm.com inurl:careers AROUND(3) AI**|Search for AI-related career pages|
|**site:ibm.com filetype:xlsx**|Look for publicly available spreadsheets|
|**site:ibm.com filetype:pdf -jobs**|Find PDF documents while excluding job-related<br>pages|



## **<u>Findings</u>** 

- Annual Reports were publicly available. 

- AI white papers and research publications were indexed. 

- Product brochures and technical documentation were accessible. 

- IBM careers pages appeared in search results. 

- Google's cached version of the homepage was available. 

- IBM publishes extensive technical documentation. 

- IBM maintains a strong public digital footprint. 

- Search engines index a large amount of IBM's official content. 

- Public reports indicate transparency for investors and researchers. 

## **<u>Activities Deliberately Avoided</u>** 

To follow ethical OSINT practices, the following actions were **not** performed: 

- Attempting to log in to any portal. 

- Accessing restricted or private content. 

- Exploiting exposed directories. 

- Downloading sensitive information. 

- Interacting with authentication pages. 

- Performing vulnerability testing. 

Only publicly available information indexed by Google was observed. 

## **<u>Search Engine Exposure</u>** 

IBM has a significant search engine presence with thousands of publicly indexed resources, including reports, research papers, documentation, career pages, and product information. This demonstrates a well-maintained and informative public web presence while supporting customers, developers, researchers, and investors. 

## **<u>Conclusion</u>** 

Advanced Google Dorking enables investigators to efficiently locate specific publicly available information. During this investigation, IBM's extensive digital footprint and publicly accessible documentation were identified using only passive OSINT techniques while adhering to ethical guidelines.
